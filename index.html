New Testament Stories

Colorfully illustrated stories from the New Testament are written on a basic reading level. This book helps parents and teachers teach the scriptures to children. They can share their testimonies and encourage children to seek their own testimonies. Includes glossaries and photographs.

New Testament Seminary Student Manual (2023)

The lessons in this manual are designed to help you achieve this objective as you study the New Testament. This manual contains 255 lessons. (You will not need to complete all of these lessons.) Most of these lessons are aligned with the Come, Follow Me scripture block that individuals and families will be studying. Other lessons are associated with doctrinal mastery. Your teacher will help you know which lessons to complete at home and which ones you will do together as a class.

BYU Religious Studies Center

The Religious Studies Center is the research and publishing arm of Religious Education at Brigham Young University. It was established in 1975 under the direction of Dean Jeffrey R. Holland Our mission is to encourage, sponsor, and publish serious, faithful, gospel-related materials. We encourage authors to submit their research for publication. We publish and market high-quality religious books and periodicals of both an academic and devotional nature.

Y Religion Podcast

Each year, Brigham Young University Religious Education professors produce hundreds of publications on subjects related to the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. This podcast brings this research into one place to enlighten the everyday seeker of truth. Interviewing the author, we discuss why the study was done, why it matters, and why the professor chooses to be both a scholar, and a disciple. This is Y Religion.

Follow Jesus Christ’s Steps With Augmented Reality Study Tool for the New Testament

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is using augmented reality technology to bring to life key events in the life and ministry of Jesus Christ in the Holy Land through related videos, articles, scriptures and images.
The new study tool coincides with the Church’s “Come, Follow Me” study of the New Testament during 2023. The resource will be available on ChurchofJesusChrist.org in 24 languages through the end of the year.

Y Religion Podcast • April '23

Episode 78: The Most Correct Book (Nick Frederick)

Nick Frederick

Joseph Smith famously said that the Book of Mormon is "the most correct book” on Earth. But what does this really mean? Was he referring to the perfection of the grammar and English in his translation? Or was something else meant? In this episode, Professor Nick Frederick, a Book of Mormon scholar himself, examines the textual variants in the Book of Mormon, highlighting the vital work done by Royal Skousen in the Critical Text Project—and how the book can be spiritually "the most correct" despite variations in its translation over the years.
